HEALING JESUS MISSION INTERNATIONAL, DENU APARCHE

The Healing Jesus Mission International Denu, currently being headed by Rev Senanu Innocent Kpormegbey has a population of Seven Hundred Souls. The church holds two services every Sunday. The first Service, which is an Ewe Service starts from 7:30am and ends at 9:30 am. The second Service starts from 10:00am to 12 noon and it's mainly English Service.
